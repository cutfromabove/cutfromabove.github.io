A Cut From Above Lawn Care

We love being your local lawn care provider; we love serving you - our honored customer.

This company was founded by Benjamin "Kuyo Benny" Jose. It started small, with very few projects every week. Now the company has grown in size and is ever-growing, now with several new employees having come and gone (See "Our Staff").

Our motto is to always put the customer first, and to only output the best of our abilities in caring for your lawn!
